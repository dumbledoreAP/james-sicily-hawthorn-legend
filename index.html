James Sicily

Hawthorn Football Club Captain & AFL Legend

About James Sicily

James Sicily is one of the AFL's premier defenders and the respected captain of the Hawthorn Football Club. Born on January 6, 1995, Sicily has established himself as an elite intercept defender known for his exceptional decision-making, piercing kicks, and inspirational leadership.

Standing at 188cm, Sicily combines physical presence with exceptional football intelligence, making him one of the most valuable players in the competition.

Career Highlights

2023 - Maiden All Australian Selection

In his first year as Hawthorn captain, Sicily earned his first All Australian blazer, topping the AFL charts with 9.9 marks per game and 26.4 disposals per game - both career and team highs.

2022 - Peter Crimmins Medal Winner

Awarded Hawthorn's best and fairest player, recognizing his consistent excellence throughout the season as a defensive stalwart and emerging leader.

Leadership & Captaincy

Appointed Hawthorn captain, Sicily leads the Moorditj Kuda leadership group, embodying the club's values and guiding the next generation of Hawks players.

Notable Performances

Round 11, 2023

43 Disposal Masterclass

Against St Kilda at Marvel Stadium, Sicily accumulated an astonishing 43 disposals, 21 of which were intercepts, earning him MVP honors and showcasing his elite ball-winning ability.

Round 21, 2023

Aerial Dominance vs Collingwood

Sicily's colossal defensive performance against the Pies earned him Round 21 MVP, highlighting his exceptional marking ability and intercept prowess.

Round 23, 2022

Western Bulldogs Victory

Sicily contributed 33 disposals (28 kicks, 5 handballs) and 17 marks in a 23-point win over the Western Bulldogs, demonstrating his all-around impact.

Round 3, 2023

Season Opener vs Essendon

Despite a tough loss, Sicily started the season strongly with 30 disposals (22 kicks, 8 handballs) and 15 marks, setting the tone for his captaincy year.

Why James Sicily is Special

What sets James Sicily apart is not just his elite statistics, but his impact on and off the field:

  • Elite Defender: Consistently ranks among the league's best in intercept possessions, marks, and defensive pressure.
  • Exquisite Kicker: His piercing kicks often tear through opposition defences, creating scoring opportunities from seemingly impossible situations.
  • Leadership: As captain, he leads by example, embodying Hawthorn's values and guiding the team through adversity.
  • Consistency: Year after year, Sicily delivers elite performances, rarely having an off game and constantly improving his craft.
  • Respect: Highly regarded by teammates, opponents, and fans alike for his professionalism, skill, and sportsmanship.
